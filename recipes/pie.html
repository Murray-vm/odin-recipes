Chicken Pie

Description

This homemade chicken pie is both easy and quick to make, making it a perfect weekday meal for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 roast chicken
  • 500ml cream
  • 1 punnet of mushrooms
  • Puff pastery
  • Parsley

steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180C
  2. Shred all the meat off the chicken
  3. Dice all the mushrooms
  4. Mix cream, mushrooms, chicken and parsly into an oven dish
  5. Cover dish with puff pastery
  6. Place into oven until pastery is golden brown
  7. Wait to cool and enjoy!
