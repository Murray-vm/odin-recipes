Nachos

Description

This is my take on nachos that are less soggy and more friendly to those with lactose intolerance.

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 1 tbls Garlic
  • 500g minced meat
  • 2 cans of beans (any type will do)
  • ! can of diced tomatoes
  • Nacho spice blend
  • Nacho Chips
  • Avo
  • Double cream yogurt
  • Salsa

steps

  1. Heat up a little oil in a deep pan
  2. fry your onion until clear
  3. Add minced meat unil cooked
  4. Add you spice mix
  5. Add tinned beans and tomatoes
  6. Reduce heat and cook till the sauce has thickened
  7. Serve your minced mix with avo, yogurt and salsa on the side
  8. Dip your chips in food and enjoy
