Stirfry Noodles

Description

This is a quick and easy meal that you can swap out most ingredients for what you have on hand.

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 tbls Garlic and ginger(optional)
  • 300g Meat (beef or chicken works best)
  • 400g Cut vegtables (frozen or fresh)
  • Soy sauce

steps

  1. Place a pot on the stove with hot water and noodles and cook till soft
  2. Heat up a little oil in a deep pan
  3. fry your onion, garlic and ginger until clear
  4. Add you meat until cooked
  5. Remove from pan
  6. Cook Vegatables until soft
  7. Mix vegtables, meat and noodles
  8. Top off with your soy sauce
  9. Enjoy
